## The Registration Path (Step by Step)

### Step 1 — Create a free Skilljar account
Go to **https://anthropic.skilljar.com** and create an account. This gives you access to the full prep catalog (13+ free courses as of April 2026), including:
- Claude 101 / Claude Code 101
- Building with the Claude API
- Introduction to Model Context Protocol (+ Advanced Topics)
- Introduction to agent skills
- Introduction to subagents
- AI Fluency: Framework & Foundations
- Claude with Amazon Bedrock / Claude with Google Cloud Vertex AI

These are the **prep** courses. They're open to anyone with no gate.

### Step 2 — Join the Claude Partner Network (required for the exam)
The exam itself is currently gated behind **Claude Partner Network** membership. Two paths:

**Option A — Your employer is (or joins) a Claude Partner.**
Large firms that committed within 72 hours of launch: Accenture, Cognizant, Deloitte, Infosys, plus smaller specialist firms (e.g., LowCode Agency). If your company is already a partner, ask your internal learning/AI team — you may qualify for the "first 5,000 partner employees" free-exam allocation.

**Option B — Your org applies to join the partner network.**
Any organization can apply. It's free to join but requires an organizational application, not an individual one. Pointer: start at https://claude.com/partners or reach out via Anthropic's partner contact form.

**Individual access as of April 2026:** Anthropic has indicated additional certification tiers are planned later in 2026 (for sellers, developers, and advanced architects), and individual/direct enrollment may expand. Verify current status at anthropic.skilljar.com before assuming partner membership is the only route.

### Step 3 — Register for the exam via Skilljar
Once you have partner network access, the exam registration link appears in your Skilljar account. Kryterion/Webassessor is **no longer the registration path** — that was the pre-launch infrastructure and is outdated.

### Step 4 — Schedule and pay
- Fee: $99 (or free under the partner allocation)
- Choose a time slot — online proctored, available in most time zones
- You'll do a system check (webcam, microphone, ID) before the exam starts

### Step 5 — Exam day
- Government ID required
- Quiet room, clean desk, no second monitor
- No notes, no docs, no external tools, no Claude access during the exam
- 60 questions in 120 minutes (2 minutes per question average)
- Scenario-based — 4 of 6 production scenarios drawn randomly
- Results delivered immediately after submission; digital badge issued by Anthropic

## What Happens After You Pass

- Digital badge from Anthropic (Credly or Anthropic-native — verify at issuance; Anthropic has shifted formats once already)
- **Valid for 6 months.** You'll need to recertify (or move to a successor tier) to keep the credential active. The short expiration is intentional — Claude Code and MCP evolve on roughly quarterly cycles, and a 2-year cert would test outdated knowledge.
- Public listing (optional) in the Anthropic-certified architect directory

## If You Fail

- Retake policy: as of launch, Anthropic has NOT published formal retake windows or fees publicly. Check the Skilljar policy page at registration.
- Industry analog: expect 14–30 day waiting periods between attempts and possible re-payment of the $99 fee.

## Common Confusions to Avoid

1. **Don't register at webassessor.com/anthropic.** This was the pre-launch Kryterion URL and is obsolete. Current registration is through anthropic.skilljar.com.
2. **Don't confuse Anthropic Academy with Claude Academy / AI Fluency.** The Academy is the umbrella learning brand; CCA-F is one specific credential within it.
3. **Prep ≠ registration.** You can take every prep course for free without being a partner. You cannot take the *exam* without partner affiliation (as of April 2026).
4. **"Partner Network" ≠ individual paid membership.** It's an organizational program. Individuals gain access through their employer's membership.
